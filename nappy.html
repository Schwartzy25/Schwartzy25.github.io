The Client

My partner - Sophie

The Problem

Sophie was running out of time in her day to get everything she neeeded to done. As a new mother who had just started a course at General Assembly (GA) there just weren't enough hours in the day as she wanted to spend as much time as possible with her family.

The Goal

To create a way for Sophie to streamline the rest of her daily tasks so that she could focus on spending time with her family and the work that came with starting a new course at GA.

My Role

As the sole UX designer on this project my role was to find the problem, create a solution and then a low-fidelity clickable prototype

Skills Used

  • User Interviews
  • Sketching
  • Wireframing
  • Prototyping
